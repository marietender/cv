Marie Tender

American Repertory Ballet Dancer

I am a dancer with American Repertory Ballet. I began dancing at age ten with Champaign-Urbana Ballet, in Champaign Illinois. I attended The Harid Conservatory from 2010-2014, where I recieved the Dance Study award and the Jeannot Cerrone award for excellence in dance performance. I have also trained with Ellison Ballet Proffesional Training Program. I joined Colorado Ballet as a studio company member in 2018, where I performed numerous roles in productions, including Swan Lake, Romeo and Juliet, and Serenade, before joining American Repertory Ballet in 2018.
